Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?
All your data (prayers, journal entries, flock, care logs) is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to any server.

Can I back up my data?
Currently the app stores data locally only. If you uninstall the app, your data will be lost. We recommend keeping important reflections noted elsewhere as a backup.

Is the app free?
Yes. Pray & Serve is completely free and open source, with no ads or in-app purchases.

Do I need an internet connection?
No. The app works entirely offline.

What are the notification reminders?
You can enable daily reminders for prayer, journaling, and checking on your flock. These are local notifications — no data is sent anywhere.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 12, 2026

Pray & Serve ("the App") is a free, open-source mobile application published by FlutterPlaza. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

1. Data Collection

Pray & Serve does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter — prayers, journal entries, flock members, and care logs — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage (shared_preferences).

2. Network Access

The App does not connect to the internet. It requires no account creation, no sign-in, and no cloud services. All fonts and assets are bundled within the App.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or tracking services.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the App, all data is permanently deleted. The App does not create backups to any external server. Device-level security (screen lock, encryption) is your primary protection for the data stored by this App.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Because no data is collected or transmitted at all, the App is safe for users of all ages.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted here with a new effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at:
https://github.com/FlutterPlaza/pray-and-serve/issues

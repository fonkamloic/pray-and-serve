✝ Pray & Serve

Your private walk with God

Pray & Serve is a free, open-source app for prayer tracking, spiritual journaling, and pastoral care. All your data stays on your device — no accounts, no cloud, no tracking.

Features

Pray — Track prayer requests with urgency levels. Mark them as answered and celebrate God's faithfulness.

Journal — Write freely about your spiritual journey. Capture reflections, scripture, and what God is teaching you.

Serve — Care for your flock. Add people, log visits, and set reminders so no one is forgotten.

FULL DESCRIPTION (Play Store / App Store, 4000 chars max)
------------------------------------------------------------
Pray & Serve is your private companion for deepening your walk with God. Track prayers, journal your spiritual journey, and care for the people in your life — all from one beautifully designed app.

PRAY
Keep a living record of your prayer life. Add prayer requests with urgency levels, mark them as answered, and watch how God moves over time. Whether it's a quick whisper or a heavy burden, every prayer has a place here.

JOURNAL
Write freely about your spiritual journey. Capture reflections, lessons, and moments of gratitude in a private journal that's always with you. Look back on entries to see how far you've come.

SERVE
Care for your flock with intention. Add the people you're shepherding, log pastoral care visits, and set reminders so no one falls through the cracks. Whether you're a pastor, small group leader, or simply someone who cares deeply for others, the Serve tab helps you love well.

DESIGNED FOR PRIVACY
Your data never leaves your device. There are no accounts to create, no cloud sync, and no tracking. Pray & Serve stores everything locally — your prayers, journal entries, and care logs stay between you and God.

BEAUTIFUL & FOCUSED
A warm dark theme with gold accents creates a calm, focused environment. Elegant typography and thoughtful design help you slow down and be present.

KEY FEATURES
- Track prayers with urgency levels (low, medium, high, urgent)
- Mark prayers as answered and celebrate God's faithfulness
- Private journaling with date-organized entries
- Manage a flock of people you care for
- Log pastoral care visits with notes
- Set reminder intervals for follow-up care
- Fully offline — no account or internet required
- Dark theme designed for quiet reflection
- Free and open source

Pray & Serve is free, open source, and built with love for anyone who wants a simple, private tool for their spiritual life.
